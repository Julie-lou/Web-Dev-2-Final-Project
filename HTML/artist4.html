Artist Spotlight

Dina Chhan

Dina Chhan is a passionate painter born and raised in the coastal city of Sihanoukville, Cambodia. Her artwork is characterized by its lively colors and fluid forms, inspired by the natural beauty of her surroundings, including the ocean and lush landscapes.

Dina incorporates elements of Khmer folklore into her pieces, creating a harmonious blend of tradition and contemporary expression. Through her art, she aims to evoke emotions and provoke thought, inviting viewers to connect with the rich cultural narratives of Cambodia while celebrating the serenity of nature.
